------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

Identify, classify, and mitigate risks associated with
design, operation, and lifecycle of OBSIDIAN-8 V3.

Each risk includes:

- Risk ID
- Description
- Severity
- Likelihood
- Mitigation
- Verification Method

------------------------------------------------------------
2. SEVERITY SCALE
------------------------------------------------------------

1 – Minor (No injury, minor inconvenience)
2 – Moderate (System interruption)
3 – Major (Hardware damage possible)
4 – Critical (Injury risk or uncontrolled motion)
5 – Catastrophic (Severe injury or irreversible damage)

------------------------------------------------------------
3. LIKELIHOOD SCALE
------------------------------------------------------------

1 – Rare
2 – Unlikely
3 – Possible
4 – Likely
5 – Frequent

------------------------------------------------------------
5. RISK REVIEW POLICY
------------------------------------------------------------

Risk register must be reviewed:

- Before Phase 2 exit
- Before scaled production
- After any Class A engineering change
- After critical field incident

------------------------------------------------------------
6. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA
------------------------------------------------------------

No catastrophic (Severity 5) risk may remain without
documented mitigation and validation.

Risks must be traceable to:

- Safety Architecture
- System Requirements
- Test Protocols

------------------------------------------------------------
7. DESIGN INTENT
------------------------------------------------------------

The Risk Register formalizes hazard awareness,
ensures mitigation tracking,
and enforces validation discipline.

Safety risk ownership is continuous,
not a one-time activity.
