3. DESIGN INTENT
------------------------------------------------------------

The Verification Traceability Matrix ensures:

- Every requirement is verifiable.
- Every design element is accountable.
- No architectural component exists without justification.
- No requirement remains untested.

Traceability is mandatory prior to scaled production.
