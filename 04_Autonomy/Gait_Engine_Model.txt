OBSIDIAN-8 V3 – GAIT ENGINE MODEL
Revision: V3.0
Status: Baseline Locked

------------------------------------------------------------
1. DESIGN PHILOSOPHY
------------------------------------------------------------

Movement must be:

- Deterministic
- Geometry-aware
- Energy-aware
- Stability-prioritized

Motion timing is isolated from AI inference timing.

Servo PWM handled by Maestro.

Gait logic runs in Jetson autonomy layer.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. PRIMARY GAIT – TRIPOD
------------------------------------------------------------

Tripod Set A:
LF, RM, LR

Tripod Set B:
RF, LM, RR

At any time:
One tripod supports full body weight.
Opposing tripod swings.

CG must remain inside support triangle.

------------------------------------------------------------
3. GAIT PHASE MODEL
------------------------------------------------------------

Phase 1 – Lift
- Lift swing tripod to defined height
- Maintain support tripod torque

Phase 2 – Swing
- Move swing legs forward
- Maintain pitch compensation

Phase 3 – Plant
- Controlled foot placement
- Confirm contact

Phase 4 – Transfer
- Shift load to newly planted tripod
- Repeat cycle

Gait frequency dynamically adjustable.

------------------------------------------------------------
4. STRIDE PARAMETERS
------------------------------------------------------------

Normal Mode:
Moderate stride length
Moderate lift height (80–120 mm)

Aggression Mode:
Increased stride length
Increased frequency
Higher current draw

Conservation Mode:
Short stride
Reduced frequency
Energy prioritization

------------------------------------------------------------
5. STAIR MODE
------------------------------------------------------------

Activated when:
Depth sensor detects vertical obstacle ≤200 mm.

Behavior:

- Reduce gait frequency
- Increase lift arc ≥200 mm
- Shift CG forward before lift
- Use body pitch compensation

Abort Conditions:

- Tilt > 10°
- Current spike beyond threshold
- Voltage sag
- Foot contact failure

------------------------------------------------------------
6. LOW-CLEARANCE CROUCH MODE
------------------------------------------------------------

Activated when:

- Low overhead clearance detected
- Energy-critical state
- Fault escalation

Behavior:

- Lower body height
- Reduce stride length
- Limit maximum pitch

Crouch is mechanically stable safe state.

------------------------------------------------------------
7. ENERGY-AWARE GAIT SCALING
------------------------------------------------------------

If voltage drops below threshold:

- Reduce aggression
- Shorten stride
- Increase stability bias

If projected energy < return reserve:

Force RETURN_TO_DOCK.

------------------------------------------------------------
8. REACTIVE INTERRUPTS
------------------------------------------------------------

Reactive layer can override gait if:

- Obstacle detected mid-swing
- Foot contact lost
- Excessive tilt detected
- Terrain instability detected

Reactive interrupt pauses gait cycle safely.

------------------------------------------------------------
9. STABILITY ENFORCEMENT
------------------------------------------------------------

Before each tripod transfer:

Verify CG projection inside support polygon.

If outside threshold:

Delay transfer.
Adjust body pitch.
Reduce stride length.

------------------------------------------------------------
10. DESIGN SIGNATURE
------------------------------------------------------------

OBSIDIan-8 gait engine:

- Never relies on servo stall torque
- Avoids full extension under load
- Adapts stride based on energy
- Prioritizes stability over speed
- Degrades safely under fault conditions
