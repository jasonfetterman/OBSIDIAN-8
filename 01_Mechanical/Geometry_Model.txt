OBSIDIAN-8 V3 – GEOMETRY MODEL
Revision: V3.0
Status: Baseline Locked

------------------------------------------------------------
1. CORE DIMENSIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

Standing Height (Neutral): 1143 mm
Target Total Mass: ≤ 20 kg
Crouch Height: ~680 mm CG reference
Maximum Stair Rise: 200 mm

Body Configuration:
Octagonal 2020 aluminum extrusion frame

------------------------------------------------------------
2. BODY STRUCTURE
------------------------------------------------------------

Frame Type:
2020 aluminum extrusion (8-sided central core)

Central Electronics Plane:
Approx. 300 mm diameter mounting plate
Material: 3 mm aluminum or 5 mm polycarbonate

Leg Mounting:
Reinforced 3–5 mm aluminum or reinforced printed plates

Structural Design Goal:
Minimize torsional flex during tripod gait.

------------------------------------------------------------
3. LEG GEOMETRY
------------------------------------------------------------

Per Leg:
3 DOF configuration

Segments:
Coxa
Femur
Tibia

Design Bias:
Long tibia for stair clearance
Moderate femur for torque efficiency
Coxa width optimized for lateral stability

------------------------------------------------------------
4. NEUTRAL STANCE GEOMETRY
------------------------------------------------------------

Neutral Stance Requirements:

- Feet positioned for maximum stability polygon
- CG centered within 50 mm of geometric center
- No servo near stall torque at neutral
- Tilt tolerance: ≤ 5° in neutral

Stance Polygon:
Must fully enclose CG projection during static posture.

------------------------------------------------------------
5. TRIPOD GAIT GEOMETRY
------------------------------------------------------------

Tripod Sets:
Set A: LF, RM, LR
Set B: RF, LM, RR

Lift Height (Normal Mode): ~80–120 mm
Lift Height (Stair Mode): ~200 mm

Stride Length:
Scaled based on voltage and performance mode.

Design Requirement:
No CG excursion outside support triangle.

------------------------------------------------------------
6. STAIR MODE GEOMETRY
------------------------------------------------------------

Front Tripod:
Increased lift arc to ≥ 200 mm

Body Pitch Compensation:
Forward CG shift before rear tripod lift.

Maximum Safe Pitch:
≤ 10° sustained during stair ascent.

Abort Conditions:
- Excessive tilt
- Current > threshold
- Voltage sag

------------------------------------------------------------
7. HEAD MODULE GEOMETRY
------------------------------------------------------------

Head Position:
Forward-biased mount above CG plane.

Contains:
- 6 RGB cameras (wide field coverage)
- 1 depth camera
- IMU
- RTK antenna mount (top surface, clear sky exposure)

Cable Routing:
Strain-relieved through protected channel to central electronics plane.

------------------------------------------------------------
8. CG TARGET MODEL
------------------------------------------------------------

Primary CG Target:
Within central octagon.

Vertical CG:
As low as practical while preserving stair clearance.

Battery Placement:
Lowest practical mounting position.

Heavy Compute Placement:
Centralized and thermally isolated.

------------------------------------------------------------
9. DESIGN CONSTRAINTS
------------------------------------------------------------

- No structural member may flex enough to disturb SLAM alignment.
- No servo horn shall be load-bearing without reinforcement.
- Bearing-supported joints required at primary torque transfer points.
- Printed components must not carry primary torsional loads.

------------------------------------------------------------
10. DESIGN PRIORITIES
------------------------------------------------------------

1. Stability under load
2. Predictable CG behavior
3. Stair safety
4. Cable protection
5. Modular serviceability

------------------------------------------------------------
END OF GEOMETRY MODEL
