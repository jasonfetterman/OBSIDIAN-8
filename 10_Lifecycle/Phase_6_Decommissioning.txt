------------------------------------------------------------
1. PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

Define controlled retirement and disposal process
for OBSIDIAN-8 V3 units.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. TRIGGER CONDITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

Decommissioning initiated when:

- End-of-life reached
- Structural integrity compromised
- Upgrade no longer feasible
- Regulatory requirement mandates retirement

------------------------------------------------------------
3. DATA HANDLING
------------------------------------------------------------

Before retirement:

- Backup logs
- Remove credentials
- Wipe storage media
- Archive configuration record

------------------------------------------------------------
4. HARDWARE DISPOSITION
------------------------------------------------------------

- Battery removed and processed per lithium regulations
- Electronics recycled per compliance requirements
- Frame components recycled or scrapped

------------------------------------------------------------
5. SERIAL RECORD UPDATE
------------------------------------------------------------

Unit record must be marked:

DECOMMISSIONED

Include:

- Date
- Reason
- Disposition method

------------------------------------------------------------
6. SAFETY REQUIREMENTS
------------------------------------------------------------

Servo rail must be permanently disabled.
Battery must be electrically isolated.

------------------------------------------------------------
7. EXIT CONDITION
------------------------------------------------------------

Unit removed from active fleet registry.
