1. PURPOSE
------------------------------------------------------------

Provide structured Failure Modes and Effects Analysis (FMEA)
for OBSIDIAN-8 V3.

Each failure mode includes:

- Failure ID
- Subsystem
- Failure Mode
- Cause
- Effect
- Severity (S)
- Occurrence (O)
- Detection (D)
- Risk Priority Number (RPN = S × O × D)
- Mitigation

Severity, Occurrence, Detection scale: 1 (Low) to 5 (High).

------------------------------------------------------------
3. RPN INTERPRETATION
------------------------------------------------------------

RPN ≥ 25:
Requires engineering review and mitigation strengthening.

RPN 15–24:
Acceptable with monitoring and validation.

RPN < 15:
Controlled risk with defined mitigation.

------------------------------------------------------------
4. REVIEW POLICY
------------------------------------------------------------

FMEA must be reviewed:

- Before Phase 2 exit
- After Class A design changes
- After critical field incident
- Annually during production

------------------------------------------------------------
5. DESIGN INTENT
------------------------------------------------------------

FMEA ensures structured evaluation of failure pathways,
prioritizes mitigation effort,
and enforces proactive engineering discipline.

Failure must be predictable and contained.
