PURPOSE

Define estimated mass distribution
and center of gravity (CG) target zone
for frame validation and stability control.

---------------------------------------
CENTER OF GRAVITY TARGET
---------------------------------------

Horizontal CG:
Must fall within 50 mm of geometric center.

Vertical CG Target:
Between 35% and 55% of FRAME_HEIGHT.

For FRAME_HEIGHT = 900 mm:
CG Target Zone:
315 mm – 495 mm above base ring.

---------------------------------------
CG CONTROL RULES
---------------------------------------

- Heavy components mounted low when possible.
- Batteries placed below mid-height.
- Symmetrical mass distribution around center.
- Avoid cantilevered mass beyond ring perimeter.

---------------------------------------
STATIC STABILITY CONDITION
---------------------------------------

Projected CG must remain inside primary ring
under 10° tilt condition.

No tipping moment under static imbalance.

---------------------------------------
CG VALIDATION METHOD
---------------------------------------

1. Assemble frame.
2. Install simulated component masses.
3. Suspend system from single lift point.
4. Mark vertical line from suspension.
5. Verify line intersects central 100 mm circle.

---------------------------------------
DYNAMIC STABILITY CONSIDERATION
---------------------------------------

Lower CG improves:
- Torsional resistance
- Landing stability
- Vibration control

Excessively low CG may:
- Increase landing shock loads on base

Balanced vertical distribution preferred.
