OBSIDIAN-8 V3
PRINTED PARTS SPECIFICATION v1
Based on: V3_LARGE_FORMAT_BASELINE
Status: STRUCTURAL INTERFACE DEFINITION
Material: Carbon Fiber Reinforced Nylon
Units: Millimeters

---------------------------------------
GENERAL PRINT PARAMETERS
---------------------------------------

Material: CF Nylon (structural grade)
Shrink Compensation Scale: 1.002 – 1.005
Layer Height: 0.2–0.3 mm
Infill: 50–70% Gyroid or Cubic
Perimeters: Minimum 5 walls
Top/Bottom Layers: Minimum 6

Minimum Structural Wall Thickness: 6 mm
Reinforced Load Zones: 10–12 mm
Insert Clearance: +0.2 mm bolt hole oversize

All structural fasteners must use heat-set threaded inserts.
No direct threading into nylon permitted.

---------------------------------------
PART GROUP A — PRIMARY RING CORNER BLOCK
---------------------------------------

Quantity: 12

Function:
Connect 3030 ring segments at 15° miter joints.

Interface:
- Accepts two 3030 ends
- M8 fastener channels
- Steel T-nut engagement

Design Rules:
- Compression transfer into aluminum faces
- Printed part only handles shear stabilization
- Internal ribbing required
- Minimum 12 mm material around bolt channels

---------------------------------------
PART GROUP B — VERTICAL POST FOOT BLOCK
---------------------------------------

Quantity: 8

Function:
Mount vertical 3030 posts to ring node.

Interface:
- Base saddle for ring segment
- Vertical receiver for 3030 post
- M8 structural fasteners

Design Rules:
- Direct aluminum-to-aluminum compression alignment
- Printed block must not carry axial compression load alone
- Add internal gussets at 45° angles

---------------------------------------
PART GROUP C — RADIAL 2020 CLAMP
---------------------------------------

Quantity: 6

Function:
Attach 2020 radial brace to 3030 primary ring.

Interface:
- 3030 slot engagement
- 2020 perpendicular capture
- M6 fasteners

Design Rules:
- Clamp geometry distributes shear
- Avoid thin arms under bending load
- Minimum 8 mm wall at junctions

---------------------------------------
PART GROUP D — CROSS BRACE GUSSET
---------------------------------------

Quantity: 16 (variable)

Function:
Stabilize 2020 cross bracing between vertical posts.

Interface:
- Dual 2020 capture channels
- M6 insert locations

Design Rules:
- Triangular geometry preferred
- Prevent torsional twist
- Maintain 6 mm minimum edge margin from inserts

---------------------------------------
PRINT ORIENTATION RULES
---------------------------------------

- Layer lines must not align with primary tensile force.
- Orient parts so compression loads are vertical relative to layers.
- Avoid printing large structural faces flat if bending stress is expected.
- Add fillets (minimum 3 mm radius) to all internal corners.

---------------------------------------
FAILURE PREVENTION RULES
---------------------------------------

- No printed part carries primary compression path.
- No cantilevered thin arms under dynamic load.
- No unsupported insert closer than 3 mm to edge.
- Test-fit every interface before final assembly.
