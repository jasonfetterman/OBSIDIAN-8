OBSIDIAN-8 V3
FASTENER SPECIFICATION v1
Based on: V3_LARGE_FORMAT_BASELINE
Status: STRUCTURAL HARDWARE DEFINITION
Units: Metric

---------------------------------------
PRIMARY STRUCTURE — 3030 CONNECTIONS
---------------------------------------

Primary Ring Joints (3030 to 3030)
Fastener Type: M8 Socket Head Cap Screw
Grade: 10.9 minimum
Length: 20–25 mm (verify with corner block depth)
Washer: M8 hardened washer
Thread Interface: Steel T-nut or threaded insert block
Torque Spec: 22–25 Nm

Vertical Post to Ring Node
Fastener Type: M8 Socket Head Cap Screw
Grade: 10.9 minimum
Length: 25–30 mm
Thread Interface: Steel T-nut
Torque Spec: 22–25 Nm

---------------------------------------
SECONDARY STRUCTURE — 2020 CONNECTIONS
---------------------------------------

2020 to 3030 Interfaces
Fastener Type: M6 Socket Head Cap Screw
Grade: 10.9 minimum
Length: 16–20 mm
Thread Interface: Steel T-nut (slot compatible)
Torque Spec: 9–11 Nm

2020 Cross Bracing
Fastener Type: M6 Socket Head Cap Screw
Grade: 10.9 minimum
Length: 16–20 mm
Torque Spec: 9–11 Nm

---------------------------------------
PRINTED CF NYLON COMPONENT INTERFACES
---------------------------------------

Heat-Set Inserts:
Type: Brass heat-set threaded insert
Size: M6 and M8 as required
Install Method: Controlled temperature insertion
Minimum Wall Thickness Around Insert: 3 mm radial

Bolt Hole Oversize:
+0.2 mm over nominal diameter

Printed Part Rule:
- No direct threading into nylon
- All structural fasteners use inserts
- Avoid tensile loading of printed material

---------------------------------------
VIBRATION CONTROL
---------------------------------------

Threadlocker:
Type: Medium strength (Blue)
Applied to all structural fasteners

Optional:
Nord-Lock washers for primary ring joints (recommended)

---------------------------------------
GENERAL HARDWARE RULES
---------------------------------------

- Do not mix imperial hardware
- Do not use low-grade (8.8 or below) for primary load paths
- Re-torque after first structural load test
- Inspect printed interfaces after first 10 operational hours
