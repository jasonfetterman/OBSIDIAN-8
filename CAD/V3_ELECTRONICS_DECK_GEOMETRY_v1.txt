OBSIDIAN-8 V3
ELECTRONICS DECK GEOMETRY v1
Based on: V3_LARGE_FORMAT_BASELINE
Status: AVIONICS MOUNTING STRUCTURE DEFINITION
Units: Millimeters

---------------------------------------
PURPOSE
---------------------------------------

Define geometry and mounting rules
for flight controller, power distribution,
communications, and sensor modules.

Electronics must:
- Be structurally stable
- Be vibration-controlled
- Remain within CG control zone
- Be serviceable

---------------------------------------
LOCATION
---------------------------------------

Primary Electronics Deck Height:
40–55% of FRAME_HEIGHT

For 900 mm frame:
Deck zone = 360 mm – 495 mm above base ring

Deck must remain within:
300 mm central clearance cylinder

---------------------------------------
DECK STRUCTURE
---------------------------------------

Support Structure:
Mounted to 2020 secondary bracing

Do NOT mount directly to:
Motor posts without bracing tie-in

Recommended Deck Plate:
Material: 3–5 mm aluminum or composite
Diameter: 180–240 mm

---------------------------------------
MOUNTING PATTERN
---------------------------------------

Deck Fasteners:
M5 or M6

Minimum 4 mounting points
Evenly spaced

Bolt spacing:
≥ 40 mm

---------------------------------------
VIBRATION ISOLATION
---------------------------------------

Electronics stack must use:

Moderate-stiffness isolation standoffs
(Shore 60–80A equivalent)

Isolation applied:
Between deck plate and electronics,
NOT between deck and frame.

Frame-to-deck must remain rigid.

---------------------------------------
COMPONENT ZONES
---------------------------------------

Zone A — Flight Controller
Centered within ±20 mm of origin

Zone B — Power Distribution
Below flight controller if possible

Zone C — Communication Modules
Upper deck region, clear line-of-sight

Zone D — Sensor Modules
Isolated from high-current wiring

---------------------------------------
WIRING CLEARANCE
---------------------------------------

Minimum wire bend radius:
5× wire diameter

No wire runs across:
Motor mounting bolts
Sharp aluminum edges

Provide:
Cable pass-through gaps in radial bracing

---------------------------------------
THERMAL RULES
---------------------------------------

Maintain airflow path
through center cylinder.

Do not enclose electronics fully
without venting.

Minimum 15 mm spacing
between high-power components.

---------------------------------------
SERVICE ACCESS
---------------------------------------

Deck must be removable
without disassembling primary ring.

No hidden fasteners under propulsion mounts.

---------------------------------------
CG CONTROL
---------------------------------------

Avoid stacking heavy batteries
above 50% frame height.

Place:
Heavier modules lower within deck zone.

---------------------------------------
EMI CONTROL
---------------------------------------

High-current wiring separated
from signal wiring by ≥ 30 mm.

Twist motor power pairs
to reduce EMI.

---------------------------------------
FAILURE CONDITIONS
---------------------------------------

Redesign required if:

- Electronics visibly oscillate under vibration
- Deck flex exceeds 2 mm under manual load
- Wire chafing observed
- Fasteners loosen during dynamic testing
