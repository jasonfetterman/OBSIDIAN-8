OBSIDIAN-8 V3
FABRICATION TOLERANCE SPECIFICATION v1
Based on: V3_LARGE_FORMAT_BASELINE
Status: MANUFACTURING CONTROL STANDARD
Units: Millimeters

---------------------------------------
PURPOSE
---------------------------------------

Define dimensional tolerances for aluminum members,
printed components, and assembly alignment.

Prevent cumulative tolerance drift in 45-inch frame.

---------------------------------------
ALUMINUM CUT TOLERANCES
---------------------------------------

3030 / 2020 Linear Cuts:
±0.5 mm maximum

Miter Cuts (15°):
Angle tolerance ±0.5°
Length tolerance ±0.5 mm

Post Height (900 mm):
±0.5 mm across all posts

Segments must be matched sets.
Do not mix unmatched tolerances.

---------------------------------------
RING DIAMETER TOLERANCE
---------------------------------------

Primary Ring Outer Diameter:
1143 mm ±1.0 mm

Measured across 3 different diameters.

Out-of-round condition:
Maximum deviation 2 mm total.

---------------------------------------
VERTICAL ALIGNMENT
---------------------------------------

Post vertical deviation:
< 0.5° from true vertical

Post height variation:
< 1 mm across all posts

Upper ring concentricity:
±2 mm relative to primary ring center

---------------------------------------
2020 BRACING TOLERANCES
---------------------------------------

Radial brace length:
Field trim recommended
Final installed tolerance ±1 mm

Cross braces:
Cut after primary structure confirmed square.

---------------------------------------
PRINTED PART TOLERANCES
---------------------------------------

General dimensional tolerance:
±0.3 mm after shrink compensation

Bolt hole oversize:
+0.2 mm over nominal

Insert seating depth:
±0.2 mm

Critical mating surfaces:
Test-fit required prior to final assembly.

---------------------------------------
FASTENER FITMENT
---------------------------------------

No forced alignment using bolts.

If hole misalignment > 0.5 mm:
Disassemble and correct root geometry.

---------------------------------------
CUMULATIVE ERROR CONTROL
---------------------------------------

Do not:
- Fully torque structure before confirming symmetry.
- Install upper ring before verifying primary ring flatness.
- Install cross bracing before vertical alignment confirmed.

Build must progress in validation stages.

---------------------------------------
INSPECTION CHECKPOINTS
---------------------------------------

Checkpoint 1:
Primary ring diameter & flatness verified.

Checkpoint 2:
Vertical posts aligned and equal height.

Checkpoint 3:
Upper ring concentric and level.

Checkpoint 4:
Full torque inspection complete.
