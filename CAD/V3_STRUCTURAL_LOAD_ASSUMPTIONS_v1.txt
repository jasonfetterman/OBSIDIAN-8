OBSIDIAN-8 V3
STRUCTURAL LOAD ASSUMPTIONS v1
Based on: V3_LARGE_FORMAT_BASELINE
Status: DESIGN LOAD DEFINITION
Units: Metric

---------------------------------------
PURPOSE
---------------------------------------

Define conservative structural load assumptions
for frame sizing and stiffness validation.

These are not operational limits.
They are structural design baselines.

---------------------------------------
ASSUMED TOTAL SYSTEM MASS
---------------------------------------

Target Estimated All-Up Mass (AUM):
40–55 kg (including propulsion, electronics, payload)

Design Mass Used for Calculations:
60 kg (conservative margin)

Gravitational Force (60 kg):
≈ 588 N

---------------------------------------
STATIC LOAD CASES
---------------------------------------

Load Case 1 — Static Ground Support
Total vertical load:
600 N distributed through ring

Load Case 2 — Uneven Ground Support
100% system mass assumed supported
by 50% of ring perimeter

Equivalent localized load:
≈ 600 N applied over half structure

---------------------------------------
DYNAMIC LOAD CASES
---------------------------------------

Load Case 3 — Vertical Shock (Landing / Impact)

Design Factor:
2.5 g vertical impulse

Equivalent load:
60 kg × 2.5 g
≈ 1470 N

This load assumed:
- Distributed across radial bracing
- Transferred through primary ring

Load Case 4 — Torsional Twist

Assume 300 N lateral force
applied at top of vertical posts.

Creates torsional moment
around center axis.

Frame must:
- Deflect elastically
- Return to neutral
- Show no permanent deformation

---------------------------------------
PRINTED PART LIMITATIONS
---------------------------------------

Printed CF nylon components:
- Not rated for primary axial compression
- Not rated for sustained tensile loads
- Rated for shear stabilization only

All major compression paths:
Must pass aluminum-to-aluminum.

---------------------------------------
DEFLECTION TARGETS
---------------------------------------

Primary ring vertical deflection under 2.5 g load:
< 3 mm

Torsional twist under 300 N lateral:
< 2 degrees

Vertical post lean:
< 1 mm deviation at top

---------------------------------------
SAFETY FACTOR TARGET
---------------------------------------

Minimum safety factor:
2.0 on aluminum members

Printed parts:
3.0 recommended due to anisotropy

---------------------------------------
VALIDATION METHODS
---------------------------------------

- Manual torsion test
- Static load test with sandbags
- Dial indicator measurement
- Post-load inspection for micro-cracking
