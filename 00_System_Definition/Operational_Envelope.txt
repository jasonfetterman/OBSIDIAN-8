OBSIDIAN-8 V3 – OPERATIONAL ENVELOPE
Revision: V3.0
Status: Baseline Locked

------------------------------------------------------------
1. PLATFORM CLASSIFICATION
------------------------------------------------------------

Hybrid Indoor/Outdoor Autonomous Octopod Mobility Platform

Primary Role:
Persistent patrol and adaptive mobility in mixed terrain environments.

------------------------------------------------------------
2. PHYSICAL ENVELOPE
------------------------------------------------------------

Standing Height: 1143 mm (3.75 ft)
Crouch Height: ~680 mm CG height
Target Mass: ≤ 20 kg
Stair Capability: 200 mm rise
Footprint: Wide octagonal stance for stability

Design Priority:
Stability > Endurance > Speed

------------------------------------------------------------
3. MISSION DURATION MODEL
------------------------------------------------------------

Target Deployment Cycle:
3+ hours (mixed-mode operation)

Mission Energy Profile:
40% Active Locomotion
40% Stationary Scanning / Sentry
20% Low-Speed Adjustment / Repositioning

Energy Reserve Rule:
Minimum 15% projected reserve required to return to dock.

------------------------------------------------------------
4. ENVIRONMENTAL CONDITIONS
------------------------------------------------------------

Indoor Operation:
- SLAM navigation
- Stair traversal
- Obstacle avoidance
- Low-light operation via depth + RGB fusion

Outdoor Operation:
- RTK GPS (centimeter-level positioning)
- Uneven terrain adaptation
- Terrain classification
- Perimeter patrol

Seamless indoor/outdoor transition required.

------------------------------------------------------------
5. TERRAIN CAPABILITIES
------------------------------------------------------------

Supported:
- Concrete
- Tile
- Carpet
- Grass
- Packed dirt
- Asphalt
- Standard staircases (≤200 mm rise)

Crouch Mode:
Activated automatically for low-clearance zones.

------------------------------------------------------------
6. PERFORMANCE MODES
------------------------------------------------------------

Normal Mode:
Balanced energy consumption and movement.

Aggression Mode:
Higher stride speed, increased lift height, higher power draw.

Conservation Mode:
Reduced stride frequency, limited stair usage.

Thermal and voltage limits always enforced.

------------------------------------------------------------
7. ENERGY ARCHITECTURE
------------------------------------------------------------

Development:
6S LiPo battery system.

Deployment:
8S LiFePO4 with Smart BMS.

Autonomous Dock:
- Visual alignment (AprilTag)
- Contact verification
- CC/CV charge profile

------------------------------------------------------------
8. SAFETY DOMINANCE RULES
------------------------------------------------------------

Safety overrides all autonomy layers.
Energy overrides mission intent.
Remote control cannot override safety or energy rules.

------------------------------------------------------------
9. DESIGN SIGNATURE
------------------------------------------------------------

OBSIDIan-8 V3 is defined by:

- Distributed compute authority
- Deterministic motion isolation
- Hardware watchdog dominance
- Energy-aware autonomy
- Persistent terrain memory
- Swarm-capable coordination
- Fail-safe posture default
